What is DigiNewsfeed?

DigiNewsfeed is an app that displays news articles of different categories for easy reading access. In total, there are five different categories by which you can filter your articles:

Economy, politics, health, climate and entertainment.

This filtering is accessible through the segment buttons just below the toolbar (note that some segment buttons are available via horizontal scrolling).

What about other features?

The app also includes the options to rate and comment on articles.

Other information such as the most recently viewed five articles, the assigned user ID and the privacy policy can be accessed via the menu in the top left corner of the toolbar.

Publishing cycle

New articles will be published every morning; the app will notify you when new articles are released. To manually see and check whether new articles are available, the list of available articles can be refreshed with a pull-down gesture on the content area of the main interface.
