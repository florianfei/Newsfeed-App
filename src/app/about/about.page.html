Welcome to using DigiNewsfeed

This app displays news articles of various topics. The interface allows you to filter by the five categories and read news articles on these various topics. The app also includes the options to rate and comment on articles. Other information such as the most recently viewed five articles, the assigned user ID and the privacy policy can be accessed via the menu in the top left corner of the toolbar.

New articles will be published every day; the app will notify you when new articles are released. To manually see and check whether new articles are available, the list of available articles can be refreshed with a pull-down gesture on the content area of the main interface.
